About Brownings Repair Co

Founded by Justin Browning, a West Virginia native with a heart to help those in need as God blesses him. Justin brings over 2 years of dedicated service to the repair and construction industry.

As a family man with a loving wife and 3 children, Justin understands the importance of having a safe, comfortable, and well-maintained home. This personal understanding drives our commitment to providing exceptional service to every customer.

At Brownings Repair Co, we believe that no job is too big or too small. Whether you need a simple repair or a complete home renovation, we approach every project with the same level of professionalism, quality craftsmanship, and attention to detail.

Our mission is simple: to serve our community with integrity, quality workmanship, and fair pricing while helping families create the homes of their dreams.
